enum UnityEngine.XR.InteractionSubsystems.GestureState

The state of a given gesture.
A gesture can be thought of as a state machine that transitions through multiple states during it's lifetime. /summary> description> Gestures are Started, then Updated while active, then either Completed or Canceled.
Some gestures however don't have a continuous lifetime, and instead have a single Discrete state. /description>
